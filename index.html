About

Kiel Paulo M. Layug

An eye for aesthetic and passion in programming, that is what motivated me in being a Full Stack Developer. Being a Computer Science graduate, I have worked with several webpages such as our thesis in college, which was a Online Blotter and Information System for one regional office of the PNP, which was built using Joomla CMS, HTML/CSS, Javascript, CodeIgniter PHP Framework and MySQL database for backend.

Information technology is evolving fast. That's why I continously learn and adapt to new technologies especially in Web Development. Additionally, I'm knowledgable with HTML 5, CSS 3, Bootstrap 5, and Gitlab.
